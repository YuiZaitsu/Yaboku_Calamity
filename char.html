HTML Caracteristics


    HTML is the most commonly used language to write webpages. there are various advantages of using HTML.


  • Provides a more flexible way to design web pages.

  • Graphics,video, and sound can be used and imported to give an attractive look to the webpages.

  • Effective presentations can be made using all formatting effects.

  • Links can be added to help readers browse additional information.

  • You can create and publish web-pages to share information with people around the world.

  • The webpages designed in HTML are widely used in marketing, for the display of products and their prices, beneficial for online shopping, and also in the advertising world.

  • You can display HTML documents on any platform such as macintosh, windows,unix,linux,etc.
